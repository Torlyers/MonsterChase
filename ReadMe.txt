Pong

The gameplay is same as the classical arcade Pong.
Press Spece to start the game and serve the ball.
Player1 uses 'w' 's' to move the pat up and down and Player2 uses 'i' and 'k'.
Once you cannot catch the ball, your opponent will get one point.Press space to reserve the ball.
If one player got five points, the game will be over. Press space to quit the game.


About the engine

Use managers and instances for every sub systems.
For every manager, use Init(), Run(), and Shutdown() to manage the loop.
Devide physics component into collider and rigidbody.
